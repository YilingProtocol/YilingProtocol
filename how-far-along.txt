How far along is your product?

MVP/demo.

The core protocol is built and working. Three Solidity contracts — PredictionMarket, MarketFactory, and FixedPointMath — implement the full SKC mechanism on-chain: market creation, bonded predictions, probabilistic stopping, cross-entropy scoring, and payout settlement. All math runs entirely on-chain with no off-chain dependencies.

We've tested 30+ markets end-to-end on Monad testnet with 7 AI agent strategies (Analyst, Bayesian, Economist, Statistician, CrowdSynth, Contrarian, Historian) that autonomously create, evaluate, and resolve markets. The mechanism works as the paper predicts — agents converge toward truthful reporting through game-theoretic pressure alone.

The project won 1st place at Monad Blitz — a competitive one-day hackathon where developers build and deploy working prototypes on Monad testnet, judged by live audience vote. Protocol documentation, landing page, and developer guides are live.

What we haven't done yet: mainnet deployment, real liquidity, or production users. The mechanism is proven in the academic literature (ACM EC 2025) and validated on testnet. The next step is mainnet launch on Monad and onboarding the first external builders.
